Bucătar la pas - Media Kit

1. About me

I’m Mihai, a chef with over 10 years of experience in international cuisine. I’ve worked in some of the top restaurants in Bucharest, with a high volume of customers and returning clients. Every time I met someone from another country — Mexico, Chile, Sri Lanka, the Philippines — I asked them to cook something traditional. I love discovering new tastes and I want to share that joy with others.

2. What is “Bucătar la pas”?

“Bucătar la pas” is a travel-cooking video series exploring Romania’s culinary roots — especially in rural and ethnic communities (Hungarians, Serbians, Lipovans, Turks, etc.). It’s about rediscovering authentic, sometimes forgotten recipes and telling the stories of the people who keep them alive.

3. Target audience

This show is made for food lovers, people passionate about authentic travel and cultural stories. Whether you're a gourmand or simply curious about Romania’s diverse heritage, you'll feel at home here.

4. Season 1: Planned episodes

  • Episode 1: Hârșova – Lipovan community
  • Episode 2: Satu Mare – Hungarian and Ukrainian traditions
  • Episode 3: Banat – Serbian & Banat cuisine
  • Episode 4: Dobrogea – Turkish-Tatar influences
  • Episode 5: Apuseni – mountain food & moți heritage
