Bucătar la pas - Media Kit

1. About Me

I'm Mihai, a chef with over 10 years of experience in international cuisine. I’ve worked in some of the top restaurants in Bucharest, serving high volumes of customers and building loyal clientele. Whenever I worked with people from other countries — Mexico, Chile, Sri Lanka, the Philippines — I asked them to cook something traditional. I love discovering new flavors and want to share that joy with others.

2. What is "Bucătar la pas"?

"Bucătar la pas" is a video series in which I travel across Romania, especially rural areas, in search of old, traditional recipes — some possibly on the brink of being forgotten. I will meet with Romanians and ethnic minorities (Hungarians, Serbs, Lipovans, Turks, etc.) and explore their culinary influences. The goal is to present food through the stories of the people who keep it alive.

3. Target Audience

We address food lovers, tradition enthusiasts, and fans of authentic travel and culinary storytelling. The show appeals to gourmets as well as the general public interested in Romanian culture and ethnic influences.

4. Season 1 Plan

  • Episode 1: Hârșova – Lipovan community
  • Episode 2: Satu Mare – Hungarian and Ukrainian traditions
  • Episode 3: Banat – Serbian and Banat cuisine
  • Episode 4: Dobrogea – Turkish-Tatar influences
  • Episode 5: Apuseni – mountain food and moți culture
